Research

In general, I study patterns of spatial and temporal change in marine ecosystems. Ongoing and recent projects include:

  • examining the consequences of fisheries closures on fisher behavior

  • understanding why some coral reefs fare better than their neighbors

  • biodiversity and body size change in coastal ecosystems, particularly in the context of recent human impacts
